Forget the message, respect the attitude

Adrian Sutton

March 7, 2005

I have to say, I am really impressed by Robert Scoble’s attitude.  Someone rips into him and unsubscribes from his feed in a highly public and critical fashion and he takes it on the chin and points out how good it is that people can do that to him (and in fact to anyone brave enough to publish their thoughts on a blog).

As someone who has ripped into Robert’s comments before (and knowing me, most likely I took the odd cheap shot at him as well in the process) and being someone that disagrees with most of what he has to say, this is why I keep reading “Scobilizer”.  He can take criticism well and actually learns from that feedback.  Sure he mightn’t go so far as coming to his senses and agreeing with me but you can always tell that he’s thought about it instead of just dismissing it off hand.

I have a lot of respect for that.
